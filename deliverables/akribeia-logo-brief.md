# Akribeia — Logo Design Brief

## The company
Akribeia is a **quantitative investment-research terminal** — it scores ~1,300 stocks across five factor pillars, runs five decorrelated trading strategies, and grounds everything in real data. Audience: serious, precise investors. Bloomberg-terminal seriousness, modern deep-tech product.

## The name
**Akribeia** (Greek *ἀκρίβεια*) means **precision, exactness, meticulous accuracy.** That is the brand idea: rigor applied to markets.

## The concept (the heart of it)
A lowercase **“a”** that is simultaneously three things in one elegant, continuous line:
- a **leaf** (organic growth), with a fine inner **vein**;
- a **growth / backtest equity curve** — the bottom of the stroke **swings back up** like a rising performance line;
- sitting on a **chart’s X / Y axes** (a small plot).

So the icon reads as: *a leaf-shaped lowercase “a,” drawn as one flowing golden line, growing off a chart axis, with the tail curving up like a winning backtest.* Precision (the name) + growth (the curve/leaf) + markets (the axes).

The icon then **leads directly into the wordmark** — the leaf-“a” is the first letter of **akribeia** (lowercase), flowing into **“kribeia.”**

> **Reference:** see the two attached reference images (the golden leaf-on-axes sketches). Match that elegant, hand-drawn, single-line quality. **The leaf + vein is the soul — do not lose it.**

## Look & feel
- **Austere, premium, deep-tech / institutional** — the register of **Palantir** and **Anduril**: serious, restrained, exact. NOT playful, NOT generic-SaaS, NOT a clip-art “eco leaf.”
- **Color:** a warm **gold** leaf (≈ `#E6C07B`) with chart axes in a muted grey, on near-black. Must also work as a single flat color and on a light background.
- **Line:** flat, fine, continuous linework — calligraphic but precise. No gradients, no 3D, no shadows.

## Wordmark
Lowercase **akribeia.** Explore a refined geometric grotesk *or* an elegant high-contrast serif — confident and minimal. The icon-“a” must sit seamlessly as the leading letter.

## Deliverables
- Primary icon (standalone) as clean **vector — SVG + editable source (AI / Figma).**
- **Horizontal lockup** (icon + wordmark) and **stacked lockup.**
- **Monochrome** (1-color) version + reversed, for light *and* dark backgrounds.
- **Favicon / app-icon** crop that still reads at 16px.

## Must-haves / watch-outs
- The **leaf + vein must survive** (most prior attempts lost it).
- The tail **swings up** (the backtest-rise) — don’t let it just drop.
- It must read clearly as a lowercase **“a” AND** as a leaf — both at once.
- **Avoid:** spiral/fiddlehead curls; a plain round bowl with no leaf; anything resembling a warning triangle or an “eye in a triangle.”
- Works **tiny** (favicon) and **huge** (hero).

## Bonus context (not required for the static logo)
The mark is also the payoff of an animated brand reveal (a star constellation that scatters and reassembles into the mark), so a **clean single-path vector** is ideal for animation.
